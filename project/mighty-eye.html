The Mighty Eye

A multi-page editorial layout designed for COMD1257 (Typographic Design) at NYCCT. This project reviews powerful voices in visual communication, including Anish Kapoor, April Greiman, Cindy Sherman, Emory Douglas, and more. The goal was to explore typographic storytelling while showcasing key figures in art and design history.
